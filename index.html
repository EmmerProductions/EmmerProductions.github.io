Started From The Bottom

With humble beginnings working in a warehouse for PSAV (Encore Global currently). I soon began picking up shifts and becoming a full-fledged technician. Having this progression has allowed me to see AV operations from all aspects. Continuing from logistics, I began crafting my technical skills, as well as the art of service, with the help of the Disney Event Group. For 4 years I was able to assist on a wide array of meetings, parties, weddings, and one of a kind, Disney internal productions!
